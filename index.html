Hello!

I’m Kenneth, a U.S. Air Force Veteran that’s passionate about expressing oneself through creativity and art. I spent my time in the military as an engine mechanic for the B1 Bomber. The knowledge and experience I got is second to none and it has helped shaped who I am today. When I wasn’t turning wrenches I was developing my other crafts; web design, web development and photography.

In February of 2021 I received my Bachelors of Science in Information Technology from Southern New Hampshire University. However, my journey with web development and design started in 2014 at the Office of Intercultural Relations in Old Dominion University. My education and learning path has truly been unconventional as well my many interests and passions in life.

When not working on web projects or taking photos, you’ll find me scouring the internet for new music or enjoying a nice cup of coffee.
